Brazil - State: Ceará - City: Fortaleza

Fortaleza is the capital of the state of Ceará, located in the northeast of Brazil. Known for its beautiful beaches, such as Praia do Futuro and Iracema, it is an important national tourist destination. The city combines a warm climate, rich culture, remarkable cuisine, and vibrant urban life. With over 2.6 million inhabitants, Fortaleza is also an economic and cultural center, highlighted by its handicrafts, forró music, and the hospitality of its people.
